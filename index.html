Cyber crimes are illegal activities that use computers, networks, or the internet as a tool, target, or both. These crimes can affect individuals, businesses, and governments.

üíª Common Types of Cyber Crimes

  • Hacking: Unauthorized access to computer systems.
  • Phishing: Fake emails or websites tricking people into giving personal data.
  • Identity Theft: Stealing personal information to commit fraud.
  • Malware Attacks: Viruses, worms, or ransomware harming systems.
  • Cyberbullying: Harassing or threatening people online.
  • Financial Fraud: Online scams, card fraud, and fake investments.
